Privacy Policy for EV Hub

Hi! Welcome to the EV Hub. We want to be super clear about how we handle your privacy, and it's pretty simple: we don't collect or store your personal information.

This policy explains how the website works and what happens with any information you interact with.

What We Collect (And What We Don't)

We built this website with your privacy in mind. This means:

  • We don't collect any personal data. We don't ask for your name, email address, or any other identifying information. There are no user accounts, so we have no way to track who you are.
  • We don't use cookies. You won't find any cookies on our site for tracking, advertising, or personalization.
  • We don't store your data. Any information you interact with on the site stays in your web browser.

How We Use Your Information (The AI Tools)

The only time any information leaves your computer is when you use the AI Tools section. When you type a question into the "Ask the EV AI" box, your text is sent to a third-party service to get a response. This process is immediate and is not stored by us.

Third-Party Services

To provide the AI functionality, we use Google's generative AI models. When you submit a question in the AI section, your query is sent directly to Google's servers.

  • Your interaction with their service is governed by their privacy policy, which you can read here: Google Privacy Policy.
  • Please note: The text you provide to the AI is used by Google to generate a response. We don't have access to or store this information.

Changes to this Policy

We might update this policy in the future to reflect any changes to the website. We'll post the new policy here with the updated date at the top.
